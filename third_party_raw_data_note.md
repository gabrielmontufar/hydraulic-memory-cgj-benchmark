# Third-party raw-data note

This package does not invent third-party experimental data. The revised manuscript uses public source records, downloaded open-access figures or supplementary files, and processed plausibility-envelope metrics. Raw third-party workbooks, time histories, or dissertation figures should be redistributed only after the license or author permission is confirmed. The Zenodo Italian clays workbook is bundled here because the record is CC BY 4.0; other raw third-party sources are not bundled.

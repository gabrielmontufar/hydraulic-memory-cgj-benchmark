# Plausibility-envelope scope note

The revised package supports a plausibility-envelope benchmark, not a calibrated constitutive law for a named soil.

Allowed claims:

- reproducible material-point benchmark;
- internally verified retention and suction-hardening components;
- quantitative ablation of hydraulic-memory and cyclic-damage effects;
- source-documented trend-consistency and plausibility-envelope checks against published unsaturated cyclic, SWCC, and dynamic-soil evidence.

Blocked claims:

- universal predictive constitutive law;
- calibrated model for all unsaturated soils;
- full site-response or earthquake-safety simulation;
- professional design approval;
- blind experimental validation.
